ABOUT ME

Hi! I'm Javier Estrela

The first time I held a camera, it felt like unlocking a new language. It's not just about clicking a button; it's about capturing a split second that tells a whole story. From the nuanced expressions on people's faces to the complex structures of buildings and the subtleties of natural landscapes, the camera allows me to spotlight the beauty in the everyday.

As a Spanish photographer currently living in Dubai, I bring a unique perspective to my work, blending cultural influences and diverse experiences to create meaningful images that resonate with a wide audience.
