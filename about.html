ABOUT ME

Hello! I'm Javier Estrela

From the moment I first held a camera, I realized it was more than just a tool—it was a key to a new form of expression. Photography, for me, is not merely about pressing a button; it's about capturing a split second that can tell an entire story. Whether it's the subtle emotions on a person’s face, the intricate architecture of a city, or the delicate beauty of nature, my aim is to highlight the extraordinary in the ordinary.

As a Spanish photographer living in Dubai, I bring a unique perspective to my work. The cultural diversity and vibrant energy of Dubai inspire me every day. My goal is to blend my experiences and artistic vision to create images that resonate deeply with my audience, particularly focusing on musicians, DJs, and artists.

Over the years, I've had the pleasure of working with a wide range of clients, capturing moments that are both personal and profound. My portfolio reflects my commitment to excellence and my passion for storytelling through the lens of my camera.
